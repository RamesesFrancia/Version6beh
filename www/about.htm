About The System

about

The study is to develop and implement “MERSy: Mobile-based Emergency Response System” to help the constituents of Olongapo City have an effective and convenient way of reporting emergency cases and incidents.

The proposed system aims to carry out the following:


1. A mobile-based safety, medical, and fire service reporting system that will not offer crime tip submittals through text, but also through video, audio, images, and surely with anonymity capabilities. about
2. A system that is accessible both online and offline. about
3. A system that will attend to all the emergency reports. about
4. A system that has an emergency mass notification system feature to be able to broadcast the messages to the users, alerting them of a pending or existing emergency. about
5. A system that has a distress alert or panic button that will submit the user’s information and current location to contact police, medics, and fire department without dialing any emergency number(s).
